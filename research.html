Research

MANARA Lab · College of Computing · UM6P

Research Interests

Trustworthy & Explainable AI
Explainable AI (XAI), trust and fairness in AI systems, blockchain-based and cross-domain recommender systems, authorship verification and stylometry.
Wireless Networks & 6G
5G/6G communications, NOMA, IRS-assisted communications, Internet of Things (IoT), resource management in wireless networks.
Security, Privacy & Blockchain
Physical layer security, trust and reputation management, federated learning with privacy guarantees, cloud forensics, decentralized systems.
Speech & Affective Computing
Speaker identification, speech emotion recognition, overlapping speech detection, multimodal audio-visual processing.
Healthcare AI & Medical Imaging
AI-driven clinical decision support, cardiovascular risk prediction, medical image analysis.
Mobile Crowdsensing
Task allocation, location privacy, crowdsensing frameworks.
